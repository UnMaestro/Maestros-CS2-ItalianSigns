## v. 1.0.0 *Autostrada del Sole* Release

## Layouts
### Added
- 147 new Layouts in different categories:
	- Autostrada Signs(Gantry Signs, Next to Lanes Signs and many more)
	- Expressway Signs(Gantry Signs, Next to Lanes Signs and many more)
	- Local Road Signs(Gantry Signs, Next to Lanes Signs and many more)
	- Signpost Signs(For every Road Categorie, there are Arrow and Post Signs)
	- Chevrons
	- More Default Signs
	- Road Rises/Road Drops Warning Signs, as well with Crossing Signs
	- Zone Signs, Parking Signs, Speed Signs
	- More Plates

### Changes
- Changed the Industry Signpost to be similar small like the new Signpost Signs
- Changed the size of Autostrada Start/End and Expressway Start/End to be smaller
- Changed the structure of Traffic Signs Folder, these have now subcategorys
